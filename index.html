What courses does CNET offer?

CNET offers a variety of courses, including IELTS preparation, web designing, and basic computer skills. These courses are designed to help learners improve their English language proficiency for the IELTS exam, develop skills in web design, and gain foundational knowledge in computer use.

Since when has CNET been offering these courses?

CNET has been offering courses in IELTS preparation, web designing, and basic computer skills since 2008. The institute has a long history of providing quality education and training in these areas.

What does the IELTS preparation course at CNET include?

The IELTS preparation course at CNET includes comprehensive training in all four sections of the IELTS exam: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. The course offers practice tests, study materials, and strategies to help students achieve their desired scores.

What is CNET and what does it offer?

CNET is a technology news and reviews website that provides information on the latest tech products, trends, and innovations. CNET also offers online courses and educational resources covering various tech topics, including programming, cybersecurity, and data science, designed to help individuals improve their skills and advance their careers.
